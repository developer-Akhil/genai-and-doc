* **AI-driven insights** improve claim accuracy, reduce processing errors, and streamline analytical and reporting workflows.

# Data Inputs & Dependencies
For the successful implementation of this solution, the following inputs are required:
* **Policy Documentation**: Medical insurance handbooks, policy guidelines, and supporting reference documents.
* **Historical Claims Data**: Prior claim records to enable pattern analysis, model training, and benchmarking.
* **Claimant Information**: Policyholder details, including demographic information, relevant medical records, and associated billing documents, where applicable.

# Architecture Overview(5 Key Steps)
**Data Ingestion & Validation:** ClaimTrackr collects claim-related data from multiple sources such as policyholder records, medical documents, external systems, and government references. The data is validated and standardized to ensure accuracy and compliance.

**Automated Data Analysis (EDA):** The platform performs automated exploratory analysis to identify patterns, trends, and anomalies within historical and current claims data, improving downstream decision quality.

**Semantic Embedding Creation:** Textual claim data, policy documents, and medical records are converted into semantic embeddings, enabling efficient comparison, retrieval, and contextual understanding of claim information.

**AI-Driven Claim Evaluation:** Using embeddings and contextual retrieval, the LLM evaluates claim validity, detects inconsistencies or potential fraud indicators, and assesses coverage against policy rules.

**Report Generation & Insights** A structured, consistent report is generated, summarizing claim status, recommendations, and settlement details, enabling faster and more informed decision-making.
